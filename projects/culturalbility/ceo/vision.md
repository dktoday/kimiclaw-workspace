# Culturalbility 文化力 - Project Kickoff

## Vision
An AI-powered cross-cultural communication training app that helps users practice conversations with culturally-diverse AI personas.

## Core Value Proposition
"Communicate cross-culturally with confidence"

## Target User (Primary)
- Chinese professional working at multinational company
- Needs to communicate with international colleagues (e.g., Indian coworkers)
- Wants to improve listening skills for different accents
- Needs to understand cultural do's and don'ts

## Key Features (MVP)
1. **AI Personas** - Diverse characters with authentic accents and cultural backgrounds
2. **Voice Conversations** - Speak and listen, not just text
3. **Cultural Coaching** - Real-time guidance on cultural appropriateness
4. **Gamification** - Streaks, progress tracking, conversation scoring
5. **Freemium Model** - Free trial, subscription for full access

## Platforms (Phased)
- Phase 1: iOS App Store
- Phase 2: Android Play Store  
- Phase 3: WeChat Mini Program
- All pointing to **single centralized database**

## Design Direction
- Minimalist flat design
- Subtly colorful (referencing Wellspoken style)
- Dark mode default with vibrant accent illustrations
- Diverse, inclusive character illustrations

## Monetization
- Freemium: Free to test
- Premium: Monthly subscription for unlimited personas/scenarios
