# App Store Optimization (ASO)

## Chinese App Stores (Priority)

### iOS App Store China
- **Developer:** Apple
- **Market share:** ~25%
- **Submission:** https://appstoreconnect.apple.com
- **Review time:** 1-3 days
- **Key:** Screenshots, description, keywords

### Android Stores (China)
| Store | Market Share | Key Strategy |
|-------|--------------|--------------|
| **Huawei AppGallery** | ~15% | Priority for Huawei users |
| **Xiaomi GetApps** | ~12% | Young demographic |
| **Oppo/vivo** | ~20% | Combined stores |
| **Tencent MyApp** | ~10% | WeChat integration |
| **Baidu** | ~8% | Search integration |

**You need to submit to ALL of them.**

## Screenshot Strategy

### Required Screenshots (iOS)
- 5 screenshots (iPhone)
- 3 screenshots (iPad, optional)

### Culturalbility Screenshot Flow

1. **Problem** - "Struggling with international calls?"
2. **Solution** - AI conversation practice
3. **Proof** - Before/after confidence scores
4. **Features** - Multiple accents, workplace scenarios
5. **Social proof** - User testimonials

### Screenshot Best Practices
- Localized text (Simplified Chinese)
- Clean backgrounds
- Value proposition in first 2 seconds
- No status bars or clutter

## Rating & Review Strategy

### Get More Reviews
1. **In-app prompt** - After positive experience
2. **Email follow-up** - 1 week after signup
3. **Incentives** - Premium features for review (within Apple rules)

### Review Response
- Reply to all negative reviews within 24 hours
- Thank positive reviewers
- Use feedback to improve

### Fake Review Warning
**DON'T:**
- Buy fake reviews
- Incentivize 5-star reviews
- Review your own app

**Apple/Google will ban you.**

## Launch Strategy

### Soft Launch (Week 1-2)
- Release in 1-2 smaller stores
- Gather feedback
- Fix critical bugs
- Optimize conversion

### Hard Launch (Week 3-4)
- Submit to all stores
- Coordinated marketing push
- PR outreach
- Paid acquisition

## Performance Tracking

### ASO Metrics
- Keyword rankings
- Impression → Page view conversion
- Page view → Download conversion
- Organic vs paid installs

### Target KPIs
- Top 10 for primary keywords
- 20%+ page view → download
- 50%+ organic installs

## China-Specific Considerations

### ICP License (✅ You have this)
Required for any app with server in China

### Content Compliance
- No politically sensitive content
- All content must be appropriate
- Regular audits

### Payment Integration
- WeChat Pay
- Alipay
- Apple Pay (iOS only)
