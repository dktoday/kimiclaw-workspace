# B2B Sales & Pilot Programs

## Enterprise Sales Process

### 1. Target Identification

**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) for Culturalbility:**
- Company size: 500-5,000 employees
- Multinational presence with China operations
- Industries: Tech, consulting, finance, pharma
- Pain point: Chinese employees struggling with international calls

**Target List Creation:**
1. LinkedIn Sales Navigator search
2. Industry event attendee lists
3. Referrals from existing contacts
4. Competitor customer analysis

### 2. Outreach Strategy

**Cold Email Template:**
```
Subject: Your team's international calls — 15 min conversation?

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] has been expanding globally. I'm reaching out because
we've built something that helps Chinese professionals communicate more
effectively with international colleagues.

Companies like [Similar Company] use us to:
• Reduce miscommunication in global meetings
• Build confidence for international presentations  
• Prepare employees for overseas assignments

Worth a 15-minute conversation?

Best,
[Your name]
```

**LinkedIn Connection + Message:**
1. Connect with relevant stakeholders (HR, L&D, Engineering managers)
2. Wait 2-3 days
3. Send personalized message about specific pain point
4. Offer value first (free assessment, industry report)

### 3. Pilot Program Design

**The 3-Month Pilot Framework:**

| Phase | Duration | Activities | Success Metric |
|-------|----------|------------|----------------|
| **Setup** | Week 1-2 | Onboarding, user selection, baseline survey | 20 users enrolled |
| **Launch** | Week 3-4 | Training, first sessions, feedback collection | 70% weekly active |
| **Measure** | Week 5-10 | Regular usage, surveys, manager check-ins | Confidence +20% |
| **Review** | Week 11-12 | ROI analysis, expansion proposal | Contract signed |

**Pilot Pricing:**
- **Free pilot** for first 3 companies (land & expand)
- Success-based pricing: ¥1,500/year per employee after pilot
- Volume discount: ¥1,200/year for 100+ employees

### 4. Stakeholder Mapping

**For Culturalbility, target these roles:**

| Role | Pain Point | How We Help | Approach |
|------|------------|-------------|----------|
| **HR Director** | Employee development budget | Measurable skill improvement | ROI focus |
| **Engineering Manager** | Team communication issues | Fewer miscommunications | Productivity angle |
| **L-6D Manager** | Training effectiveness | Analytics and reporting | Data-driven |
| **CFO/Finance** | Training ROI | Cost vs. value metrics | Business case |
| **Employee** | Personal confidence | Safe practice environment | Individual benefit |

### 5. Demo Structure

**15-Minute Demo Flow:**

1. **Problem** (2 min) — "Ever had a call where you didn't understand?"
2. **Solution** (3 min) — Show AI conversation practice
3. **Evidence** (3 min) — Case study or metrics
4. **Pilot** (4 min) — "Let's run a 3-month pilot"
5. **Next Steps** (3 min) — Schedule follow-up with decision maker

**Live Demo Tips:**
- Use real scenario (Indian colleague, stand-up meeting)
- Show both success and "try again" flows
- Share screen of analytics dashboard
- Have testimonial ready

### 6. Handling Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We use Duolingo" | "Duolingo teaches vocabulary. We teach workplace communication with your actual colleagues' accents." |
| "Too expensive" | "One miscommunication in a client call costs more than annual subscription." |
| "No budget this quarter" | "Pilot is free. Let's prove ROI, then find budget together." |
| "Security concerns" | "We use enterprise-grade encryption. Happy to share security docs or do a call with your IT team." |
| "Employees won't use it" | "Our gamification shows 85% weekly engagement. Plus, we train managers to encourage participation." |

### 7. Closing the Deal

**Signs they're ready to buy:**
- Asking about implementation timeline
- Requesting contract terms
- Introducing you to finance/procurement
- Discussing rollout plans

**Trial close questions:**
- "If the pilot goes well, when would you want to roll out company-wide?"
- "What would need to happen for this to be a priority this quarter?"
- "Who else should be involved in the decision?"

- Outreach → Meeting rate (target: 15%)
- Meeting → Pilot rate (target: 40%)
- Pilot → Contract rate (target: 70%)
- Average deal size
- Sales cycle length
