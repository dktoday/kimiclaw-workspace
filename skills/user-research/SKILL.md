# User Research

## User Interview Templates

### For Culturalbility - Target: Chinese Tech Workers

**Screening Questions (5 min):**
1. What industry do you work in?
2. How often do you have calls with international colleagues?
3. What nationalities do you work with most? (Indian/American/British/Others)
4. Have you ever felt you didn't understand someone on a work call?

**Main Interview (30 min):**

**Opening:**
"Tell me about the last time you had a challenging work call with an international colleague."

**Pain Points:**
- What was difficult about that call?
- How did you feel during/after?
- What did you do to prepare?
- What would have helped?

**Current Solutions:**
- How do you currently prepare for international calls?
- Have you tried any language learning apps? (Duolingo, etc.)
- What worked/didn't work?

**Desired Outcome:**
- If you could wave a magic wand, how would you improve your communication?
- What would make you feel confident on these calls?

**Closing:**
- Would you be interested in trying a solution that helps with this?
- What would make you pay for it?

## Feedback Analysis

### Thematic Analysis Process

1. **Collect** all feedback (interviews, surveys, support tickets)
2. **Code** - Tag each piece of feedback with themes
3. **Cluster** - Group similar codes together
4. **Prioritize** - Frequency × Impact

### Example for Culturalbility

| Feedback | Theme | Frequency | Priority |
|----------|-------|-----------|----------|
| "Indian accent is hard" | Accent difficulty | High | Critical |
| "Don't want to sound stupid" | Confidence | High | Critical |
| "No time to practice" | Time constraints | Medium | High |
| "Want to see progress" | Gamification | Medium | Medium |

## Survey Templates

### Product-Market Fit Survey

**Question:** "How would you feel if you could no longer use Culturalbility?"
- Very disappointed
- Somewhat disappointed
- Not disappointed
- N/A - I no longer use it

**Target:** 40%+ "Very disappointed" = strong PMF

### Net Promoter Score (NPS)

**Question:** "How likely are you to recommend Culturalbility to a colleague?"
- Scale 0-10
- 9-10 = Promoters
- 7-8 = Passives
- 0-6 = Detractors

**NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors**

## Validation Checklist

Before building, validate:

- [ ] 10+ user interviews conducted
- [ ] 3+ distinct personas identified
- [ ] Clear pain point confirmed
- [ ] Willingness to pay established
- [ ] Current solution inadequacy proven
