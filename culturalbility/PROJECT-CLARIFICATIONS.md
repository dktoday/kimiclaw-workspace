# Culturalbility 文化力 — Project Clarifications

## What We Are NOT Doing

❌ Teaching Chinese people to SPEAK with Indian/foreign accents  
❌ Promoting stereotypes or mockery  
❌ Casual conversation practice  
❌ WeChat Mini Program (MVP)

## What We ARE Doing

✅ Teaching LISTENING skills to understand foreign colleagues  
✅ Professional workplace communication  
✅ iOS native app (MVP)  
✅ B2B first — multinational company employees  
✅ Focused niche: Chinese tech workers with international colleagues

## Core Value Proposition

**Problem:** Chinese professionals struggle to understand their Indian, American, and British colleagues on work calls.

**Solution:** AI-powered listening practice with realistic workplace scenarios.

**How it works:**
1. User hears AI persona speak (workplace context)
2. User can replay, slow down, see transcript
3. User practices comprehension
4. AI provides feedback on understanding

## WeChat Mini Program Strategy

**Post-MVP only.**

**Use case:** Reference, conversation history, statistics
**NOT for:** Real-time practice (60-second limit is fine for reference)

**Hybrid approach:**
- Native app: Full practice sessions
- Mini Program: Quick review, progress checking, sharing scores

## B2B Focus

**Target:** Multinational HR departments in China

**Ideal customers:**
- Tech: Alibaba, Tencent, ByteDance (global teams)
- Consulting: McKinsey, BCG, Deloitte China
- Finance: HSBC, Standard Chartered, foreign banks
- Pharma: Roche, Novartis, Pfizer China

**Pricing:** ¥800-1,500 per employee/year

**Pilot plan:**
- 3 companies, 20 employees each
- 3-month free trial
- Measure: confidence scores, call quality feedback

## Tech Constraints Accepted

✅ 60-second recording limit (sufficient for our use case)  
✅ iOS first, Android second  
✅ AI costs managed via B2B pricing  
✅ 3 persona types: Indian, American, British (professional context)

## Cultural Sensitivity

- All personas vetted for accuracy by cultural consultants
- Focus on professional workplace scenarios only
- No casual/cultural stereotype content
- Regular review of AI-generated content
